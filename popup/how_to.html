How to Use the Gmail Filter Extension

Sign In

To begin, sign in and grant us permission to access your emails. This allows us to personalize your experience.

Pre-Train

Before we begin, you’ll need to complete a one-time setup:

  • Tell us your preferences: Submit a short description of the types of emails you’d like to prioritize. For example: “Emails about upcoming deadlines for bills, rent, and utilities.”
  • Submit your recent emails: We’ll request your 500 most recent emails to build your personalized vocabulary. Once processed, only the vocab is saved, not your emails.

After this one-time setup, you’ll use Train to improve your model over time.

Train

Use this regularly to refine your filter:

  • You’ll be redirected to your read emails.
  • A popup will ask how many emails you'd like to label.
  • Once confirmed, small circular buttons will appear next to each email—use these to mark which ones are a priority.
  • Click Submit when you're done labeling.

During this process:

  • A Gmail label called “Priority” will be created.
  • Once training is over, you’ll be redirected to that label to view all emails your model marked as important.
  • Emails used for training will be deleted. Only the trained model is stored.

You can return to Training Mode anytime to keep improving your model as your email habits change.

Erase

Want to start fresh? Click Erase to permanently delete all your data from our servers. Use this if your email habits or preferences have changed significantly.
